## ğŸ® How to Play

1.  **Setup:** Open the game on a tablet or laptop. If you are in a group, cast the screen to a TV so everyone can see.
2.  **Teams:** Create 2-4 teams (e.g., "The Elves" vs. "The Reindeers").
3.  **Gameplay:**
    *   **The Artist:** One person from the active team comes up to the device.
    *   **The Word:** They tap "Reveal Secret Word" (no peeking by the audience!).
    *   **The Draw:** They have 60 seconds to draw the word on the digital canvas.
    *   **The Guess:** Their team shouts out guesses. If they get it right, hit the "Correct!" button.
4.  **Winning:** The game continues for the set number of rounds. The team with the most points wins the Christmas Cup!
